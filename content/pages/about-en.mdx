About Us
At JH Car Rental, we believe every journey deserves to be handled with meticulous care. Our story began in 1988, under our former name, Dashinchu Car Rental.
We started by serving the thriving businesses of the Hsinchu Industrial Park. As the region grew, so did we. We steadily expanded our fleet and refined our services to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
________________________________________
Following the COVID-19 pandemic, we seized the opportunity to redefine our mission and strategy. This led to a pivotal rebranding, and we officially became JH Car Rental. This change was more than just a new name; it was a commitment to an elevated standard of service. We maintained our premium fleet of luxury vehicles and sharpened our focus on providing impeccable chauffeured services for corporate and high-end clientele.
Over the past two years, we've achieved significant milestones. We are honored to be a trusted partner of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, assisting in the reception of international dignitaries. This experience has not only refined our service to an international level but has also solidified our position as a leader in premium chauffeured transport. This expertise allows us to cater to the most discerning clients, and we've become the preferred service provider for luxury and jewelry brands when hosting their VIP guests.
For the Hsinchu tech community, we are the partner of choice. We understand the critical need for efficiency and privacy. From daily chauffeuring for executives and transporting key clients to seamless airport transfers for international travel, our team provides reliable and highly confidential services. Our commitment to punctuality and our chauffeurs' professionalism ensure every business trip is smooth and worry-free.
At JH Car Rental, our mission is to deliver flawless journeys for you and your important guests. Your satisfaction is the driving force behind everything we do.
